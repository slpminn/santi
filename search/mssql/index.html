Affordable Fast Responsive Accurate Custom Search (AFRA.CS)

Why AFRA.CS?

  • AFRA.CS increases your customers/viewers engagement with your content by allow them to find your content easily and fast.

  • AFRA.CS enables you to guide your customers/viewers to the most relevant content available on your website.

  • AFRA.CS enables you to control and enhance the website user experience and deliver greater relevance to increase customer engagement.

  • AFRA.CS enables you to increase the number of pages serve by your website, hence increasing your ad revenue.

Mission statement of AFRA.CS

  • To provide an affordable internal custom search by utilizing reliable and well used open source software on the cloud.

  • To provide a fast internal custom search by limiting the functionality to the core function. Returning results.

  • To provide a responsive internal custom search by using the responsive work frame Bootstrap.

  • To provide an accurate internal custom search by indexing every single word used on the story.
